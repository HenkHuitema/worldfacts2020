About

"World Facts" is an encyclopedia of countries, showing facts about 260+ countries, including the 50 states of the USA..

"World Facts" is an ad-free application, based on recent 2020 data from a.o. the popular CIA WORLD FACTBOOK, US Census bureau, etc..

For each country and US-state, multiple topics are off-line available to provide you up-to-date facts, maps, graphs and figures..

Easy Navigation

Easy (colored) menu based navigation. Available options include: World "entities" (e.g. countries, oceans, etc.), recent earthquakes, visited countries, the 50 states from the USA, 60+ rankings of countries, list of 100+ currencies and 200+ international organisations.

Easy Searching

Searching for an entity (country/ocean/etc.) can be based on name. Filtering based on region/continent makes searching even easier.

Structured Information

Information is categorised per topic for easy searching. When available/applicable these topics include an interactive map, photos, introduction, information about geographic, people & society, economy, goverment, energy, transport, communication, military/security, etc.

Up-to-date Facts

Presented facts are based on the popular CIA WORLD FACTBOOK.
(The World Factbook is prepared by the Central Intelligence Agency and is one of the US Government's most accessed publications. The Factbook presents the basic realities about the world in which we live. These facts are shared with the people of all nations in the belief that knowledge of the truth underpins the functioning of free societies. It provides information on the history, people, government, economy, geography, communications, transportation, military, terrorism and transnational issues for 267 world entities. )

Recent Earthquakes

Shows list of recent (last hour/day/week/month) earthquakes. Sortable by time or magnitude of the earthquakes.

USA State Facts

Facts categorized into multiple topics. Based on data published by the US Census bureau
